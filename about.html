About us


Raw Plumber's story has been one of constant improvement and year on year growth since its inception in 1979, with returning customers accounting for the majority of the more than 100,000 jobs completed annually.

Today, the company - the largest independent service & maintenance company in the capital - operates out of a high-tech 30,000 plus sq ft London headquarters, where the call centre is manned 24/7, 365 days a year and serves customers in and around the M25. In addition, there are several railway arches adding over another 20,000 square feet to the total. A multi-skilled, multi-trade workforce of more than 300, with fully equipped vehicles, providing both domestic and commercial customers an unequalled response.

Continued success and growth, with a turnover of in excess of £50 million and an ongoing development strategy, means Raw Plumber will undoubtedly remain at the forefront of the building services industry and as the first choice for customers who appreciate high quality service and value for money.
